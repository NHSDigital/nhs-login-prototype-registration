Where you can use your NHS login

Did you know you can use your NHS login to access lots of other health and care websites and apps too?

See the full list of services that allow NHS login.

What you can do with your NHS login

At the moment you have an NHS login with a verified email address and phone number. With this, you can do simple tasks like read general medical information or book a COVID-19 test.

To access all areas of other services, you may need to be matched to your NHS record. When you do this you will be able to do more things, like contact your GP.

If you want to access your health records or personal information on any of the services you visit, you will need to prove who you are.

What to do next

You do not need to do anything right now. If you visit a service and you are asked to prove who you are, or link up to your NHS record, we will tell you how step by step.
