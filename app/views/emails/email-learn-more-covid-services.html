Information:

We have sent you this email because you have set up your NHS login. You may have done this when you ordered a lateral flow test or booked a PCR test on the Covid-19 Digital testing service.

Right now, your NHS login has a verified email address, password, and phone number. This allows you to do basic tasks, like read your general medical information or book further COVID-19 tests.

If you can prove who you are or be matched to your NHS record, you will be able to do a lot more. You will get full access to the NHS App and a wider choice of other digital health and care services.

See the full list of websites and apps that you can access with NHS login.

Do more with the NHS App

Owned and run by the NHS, the NHS App is a simple and secure way to access a range of NHS services on your smartphone or tablet.

With full access to the NHS App you can:

  • book and manage GP appointments
  • order repeat prescriptions
  • view your health record securely
  • demonstrate your COVID-19 status using the NHS COVID Pass

Find out more about the NHS App at nhs.uk/nhs-app.

Or you can visit the App Store or Google Play and search NHS App.
