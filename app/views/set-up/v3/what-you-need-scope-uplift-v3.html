Set up an NHS login

To create an NHS login you will need your mobile phone, as we will text you a security code.

You also need to prove who you are. You can do this using your photo I.D. and a video selfie, or registration details from your GP surgery’s online services.

Prove who you are

Photo I.D. and video selfie

Accepted types of photo I.D. are:

  • Passport
  • UK driving licence
Other accepted ID types
  • European driving licence
  • European national identity card

This will take around 20 minutes. This may take longer if you are using assistive technologies.

Or

Registration details

Registration details from your GP surgery’s online services include:

  • Linkage key (could be called a Passphrase)
  • O.D.S. Code (could be called an Organisation Code or Practice I.D.)
  • Acccount I.D.

This will take around 10 minutes. This may take longer if you are using assistive technologies.
