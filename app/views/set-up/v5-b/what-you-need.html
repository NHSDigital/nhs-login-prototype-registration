What you need

5 to 10 minutes of your time

This will take longer if you are using assistive technologies.

Personal information

So that we can match you to your NHS record, you will need to tell us your NHS number and date of birth.

If you do not know these details, you can tell us your:

  • name
  • date of birth
  • postcode

A mobile phone

You will need a working mobile phone with signal, as we will text you a security code.
