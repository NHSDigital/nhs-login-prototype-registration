Enter your NHS number

Before changing your mobile phone number, you need to prove who you are. You can do this by entering your NHS number.

How to find your NHS number

An NHS number is a 10 digit number, like 485 777 3456.

You can find it:

  • on any letter the NHS has sent you
  • on a prescription
  • by logging in to your GP surgery online service
  • on a vaccination letter

You can also use the Find your NHS number service or ask your GP surgery for help if you cannot find your NHS number.
