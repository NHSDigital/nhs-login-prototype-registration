Do you know your NHS number?

Telling us your NHS number will give us the best chance of matching you to your NHS record.

You do not need to know your NHS number to continue. If you do not know it, you can tell us your full name instead.

How to find your NHS number

An NHS number is a 10 digit number, like 485 777 3456.

You can find it:

  • on any letter the NHS has sent you
  • on a prescription
  • by logging in to a GP surgery online service
  • on a vaccination letter

Ask your GP surgery for help if you cannot find your NHS number.

Example: 485 777 3456
