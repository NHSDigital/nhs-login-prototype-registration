Do you know your NHS number?

Your NHS number is a 10 digit number, like 485 777 3456.

You can find it:

  • on any letter the NHS has sent you
  • on a prescription
  • by logging in to a GP surgery online service
  • on a vaccination letter

Ask your GP surgery for help if you can't find your NHS number.
